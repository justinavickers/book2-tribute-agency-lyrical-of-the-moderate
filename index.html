GET TO KNOW LES CLAYPOOL

Les Claypool is an American musician, singer, songwriter, composer, author and actor best known as the bassist and lead vocalist of the band Primus. He has also self-produced and engineered his solo releases from his own studio, "Rancho Relaxo". In 2006 he released his film Electric Apricot which he wrote and directed as well as his debut novel South of the pumphouse. He is also responsilbe for the theme songs of Robot Chicken and South Park.

SKILLS

A man of many skills and talents. He is an avid fly fisher and has been featured twice on Fly Fishing the World. He has a boutique wine business, Claypool Cellars, and names the wines based on his music.

COLLABORATORS

He has worked with many artist for his music and theirs, including Tom Waits, Metallica, Gov't Mule and Beats Antique.

WHERE HE HAS LIVED

He has lived in Richmond, California and El Sobrante, California.
